# Pattern: Plan a Product Launch End-to-End

> Domains: Software Engineering + Business Strategy + Content Publishing + Productivity

**Principles used**: Chain-of-Thought Scaffolding, Context Layering

---

## When to Use
- Launching a new product, feature, or major update
- Need to coordinate technical, marketing, and operational work
- First launch or want to be more structured than "push to prod and tweet"

## The Pattern

```
I'm launching: {{PRODUCT/FEATURE}}
Launch date: {{DATE — or "flexible"}}
Team: {{WHO_IS_INVOLVED}}

Plan the launch across all dimensions:

TECHNICAL:
- What must be production-ready by launch? (Feature-complete list)
- What's the rollout strategy? (Big bang / gradual / feature-flagged?)
- What monitoring/alerts need to be in place?
- What's the rollback plan?

MARKETING:
- Who are the first users and how do they find out? (Channel-specific tactics, not "social media")
- What's the launch content? (Blog post, video, changelog, email, social — with timeline)
- What's the hook — why should anyone care on launch day?

OPERATIONAL:
- What support needs to be in place? (FAQ, docs, response plan for issues)
- What are the success metrics for day 1, week 1, month 1?
- What's the post-launch plan? (Feedback collection, iteration cycle, growth plan)

Put this in a timeline:
- T-2 weeks: {{WHAT_SHOULD_BE_DONE}}
- T-1 week: {{WHAT_SHOULD_BE_DONE}}
- Launch day: {{HOUR_BY_HOUR_PLAN}}
- T+1 week: {{POST_LAUNCH_ACTIONS}}

What's the ONE thing that could derail this launch? Mitigate it.
```
