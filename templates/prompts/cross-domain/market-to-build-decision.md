# Pattern: Research a Market to Decide Whether to Build

> Domains: Learning & Research + Business Strategy + Decision Frameworks

**Principles used**: Chain-of-Thought Scaffolding, Specificity Amplification

---

## When to Use
- Evaluating a product idea before committing engineering resources
- Need to validate market demand, not just technical feasibility

## The Pattern

```
I'm considering building: {{PRODUCT_IDEA}}

Before I write any code, I need to answer:
1. Is there a real problem? (Who has it, how painful is it, how are they solving it today?)
2. Is there a market? (How many potential customers, what would they pay, is the market growing?)
3. Can I win? (What's my advantage over incumbents and the status quo?)
4. Should I build it? (Given 1-3, is this worth my time and resources?)

For each question:
- What evidence would confirm a YES?
- What evidence would confirm a NO?
- What's the fastest way to gather that evidence? (Days, not months.)

End with: Go / No-go / Need more data — and if "need more data," specify exactly what data and how to get it.

Do NOT: Default to "it depends" or "more research needed" without specifying what research and how long it would take.
```
