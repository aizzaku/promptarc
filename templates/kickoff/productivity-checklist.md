# Productivity Kickoff Checklist

Extends `universal-checklist.md`. Asked after domain detection identifies a productivity/planning project.

---

## Current State

1. **What's the current system?** (No system | Basic todo list | Full GTD/other methodology | "Various scattered tools")
2. **What's the main bottleneck right now?** (Too many things | Can't prioritize | Can't start | Can't finish | No clarity on goals | Energy/motivation | Time fragmentation)
3. **What tools do you currently use?** (Calendar, task manager, notes app, etc. — or nothing structured)
4. **What's been tried before that didn't work?** (Previous systems, habits, tools — and why they failed)

## Context

5. **What's the primary role?** (Solo founder | Employee | Student | Freelancer | Manager | Mixed)
6. **What does a typical day look like?** (Meeting-heavy | Deep work blocks | Reactive/interrupt-driven | Varies wildly)
7. **What energy pattern do you have?** (Morning person | Night owl | Post-lunch dip | Consistent throughout | Don't know)
8. **What are the non-negotiable commitments?** (Meetings, family, health, commute — things that can't move)

## Goals

9. **What's the #1 thing you want to improve?** (Focus on ONE thing, not five.)
10. **What does "productive" mean to you?** (Shipping more | Less stress | Better quality | More free time | Clearer priorities | Getting the right things done)
11. **What's the time horizon?** (This week | This month | This quarter | Building a long-term system)
12. **What's the accountability structure?** (Self-motivated | Need external accountability | Have a team/manager | Need to set one up)

---

## Conditional

### If goal setting
13. **What are the current goals?** (Stated or unstated. If unstated, setting them is the first task.)
14. **What's the measurement?** (How will you know progress is happening?)

### If habit design
15. **What habit(s) are you targeting?** (Be specific — "exercise more" → "30-min walk at 7 AM Mon/Wed/Fri")
16. **What's the trigger/cue?** (What existing routine can this attach to?)
17. **What's the failure mode?** (When you've failed at this before, what happened?)

### If project breakdown
18. **What's the project?** (Full description of the overwhelming thing that needs decomposition)
19. **What's the next concrete action you could take right now?** (If you can answer this, you may just need accountability, not decomposition.)

### If system design
20. **What's the ideal weekly rhythm?** (Deep work blocks, meeting days, admin time, buffer)
21. **What's the review cadence you'll actually do?** (Daily? Weekly? Be honest — aspirational cadences fail.)
