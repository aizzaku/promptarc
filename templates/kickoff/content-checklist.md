# Content Publishing Kickoff Checklist

Extends `universal-checklist.md`. Asked after domain detection identifies a content project.

---

## Audience & Voice

1. **Who specifically reads/watches this?** (Not demographics — describe one real person. What do they already know? What do they care about?)
2. **Paste a paragraph you've written that sounds like YOU.** (Voice sample for calibration. If none available, name a writer whose style you want to match.)
3. **What tone makes you cringe?** (Anti-voice signals. "Too corporate," "too casual," "too clickbaity," etc.)
4. **What's the reader's sophistication level?** (Beginner | Intermediate | Expert | Mixed)

## Content Details

5. **What's the content type?** (Long-form article | Blog post | Video script | Social thread | Newsletter | Documentation | Presentation | Other)
6. **What's the target platform?** (Personal blog | Medium | Substack | YouTube | Twitter/X | LinkedIn | Company blog | Other)
7. **What's the target length?** (Word count, video duration, or thread length)
8. **What's the angle?** (Not the topic — the specific argument, perspective, or hook. If undecided, ideation is the first task.)

## Pipeline

9. **Where are we in the pipeline?** (Ideation | Research | Outline | Draft | Edit | Polish)
10. **What existing material do we have?** (Notes, rough drafts, research links, prior articles on the topic)
11. **What's the publication cadence?** (One-off | Weekly | Daily | Series)

## Distribution

12. **Is SEO a concern?** (Target keywords? Competitive articles to outrank?)
13. **Does this need visual assets?** (Images, diagrams, thumbnails, charts)
14. **What's the CTA?** (What should the reader do after consuming this? Subscribe? Buy? Share? Think differently?)
15. **How will this be distributed?** (Organic | Email list | Social promotion | Paid | Cross-posting)

---

## Conditional

### If video script
16. **What's the format?** (Talking head | Screencast | Essay-style voiceover | Interview | Tutorial)
17. **What's the hook structure?** (Cold open? Teaser question? Bold claim?)
18. **B-roll or visual cues needed?** (What should be on screen when not showing the speaker?)

### If series/recurring content
19. **What's the series arc?** (How do pieces connect? Is there a progression?)
20. **What's the differentiation from prior pieces?** (Why is THIS piece necessary given what you've already published?)

### If ghostwriting/client work
21. **Who is the byline?** (Writing as yourself or as someone else?)
22. **Is there a brand style guide?** (Voice, terminology, formatting rules from the client)
