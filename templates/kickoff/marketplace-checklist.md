# Marketplace Kickoff Checklist

Extends `universal-checklist.md`. Asked after domain detection identifies a marketplace project.

---

## Model

1. **What's being exchanged?** (Physical goods | Services | Digital goods | Time / bookings | Rentals)
2. **Who are the two sides?** (Buyer persona and seller/provider persona — be specific, not just "users")
3. **Take rate model?** (Percentage of GMV | Subscription fee | Lead fees | Freemium with paid features)

---

## Cold Start

4. **Which side will you build first?** (Usually supply — state the reason if going demand-first)
5. **Minimum supply threshold before demand side is viable?** (What does "enough supply" look like?)
6. **Geographic or category focus to concentrate initial liquidity?** (Single city? Single vertical? Broad from day one — and why?)

---

## Trust & Safety

7. **Identity verification for providers?** (None | Email/phone | ID verification | Background check)
8. **Review system?** (Bidirectional | Buyer-only | Post-transaction only | No reviews — and why)
9. **Dispute resolution process?** (Who handles it, by what rules, with what SLA)
10. **Payment escrow or immediate release?** (Escrow until fulfillment confirmed is the default — why diverge?)

---

## Liquidity

11. **How will you measure liquidity?** (Search-to-booking rate | Fill rate | Time-to-match | Other)
12. **Category or geographic constraint to concentrate early liquidity?**

---

## Leakage

13. **What prevents buyers and sellers from transacting off-platform after the introduction?**
14. **What platform value persists post-connection?** (Payments | Insurance | Reviews | Repeat booking | Dispute resolution)

---

## Conditional

### If services marketplace
15. **Provider vetting or background checks required?** (What's the bar and who runs it?)
16. **Insurance or liability coverage offered by the platform?**

### If physical goods marketplace
17. **Shipping and logistics: platform-managed or seller-managed?**
18. **Returns and refunds policy owned by platform or individual sellers?**
