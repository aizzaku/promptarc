# Education Kickoff Checklist

Extends `universal-checklist.md`. Asked after domain detection identifies an education technology project.

---

## Audience & Market

1. **Target learner?** (K-12 | Higher education | Professional / corporate | Consumer / informal learning)
2. **Age range of primary users?** (Under 13 triggers COPPA — confirms consent model needed)
3. **Who buys the product?** (Student / consumer | Teacher | School / district administrator | Employer / L&D team)
4. **Sales motion?** (Self-serve teacher signup | District procurement | Consumer direct | Enterprise / employer)

---

## Compliance

5. **FERPA requirements?** (Applies to US schools receiving federal funding — student educational records have strict handling rules)
6. **COPPA compliance needed?** (Any users under 13 — parental consent or school-as-operator safe harbor required)
7. **Accessibility standard?** (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum for school procurement — which level is targeted?)
8. **State student data privacy laws?** (SOPIPA, NY Ed Law 2-d, Illinois SOPPA — which states are relevant?)

---

## Learning Design

9. **Instructional model?** (Self-paced asynchronous | Live cohort | Blended | Adaptive / personalized)
10. **Assessment and credentialing?** (Quizzes | Formal grades | Certificates | Badges | None)
11. **How will learning outcomes be measured?** (Not just completion rates or time-on-task — what's the outcome metric?)

---

## Integrations

12. **LMS integration required?** (Canvas | Google Classroom | Schoology | Blackboard | Moodle | None)
13. **LTI support needed?** (LTI 1.3 for SSO + grade passback — required for most district procurement)
14. **Rostering integration?** (Clever | ClassLink | CSV import | Manual teacher-managed | None)

---

## Content

15. **Content created by platform, teachers, or learners?** (Or mixed — affects moderation requirements)
16. **Video content?** (Hosting | Transcoding | Captioning requirement — WCAG requires captions)
17. **Curriculum alignment needed?** (Common Core | State standards | IB | AP — which frameworks?)

---

## Conditional

### If K-12 schools
18. **FERPA data processing agreement (DPA) available?** (Districts require it before any student data sharing)
19. **District-level procurement process expected?** (Security review | Privacy review | IT approval | Pilot requirement)

### If under-13 users
20. **Consent model?** (COPPA parental consent flow | School-as-operator safe harbor via signed agreement)
21. **Data minimization confirmed?** (Collect only what's necessary for the educational service)
