# Business Strategy Kickoff Checklist

Extends `universal-checklist.md`. Asked after domain detection identifies a business strategy project.

---

## Company & Stage

1. **What stage is the company/project?** (Idea | Pre-revenue | Early revenue | Growth | Mature | Turnaround)
2. **What's the business model?** (SaaS | Marketplace | E-commerce | Services | Ad-supported | Hardware | Other | Undecided)
3. **What's the current revenue?** (None | <$10K MRR | $10K-100K MRR | $100K-1M MRR | $1M+ MRR | Not applicable)
4. **What's the funding status?** (Bootstrapped | Pre-seed | Seed | Series A+ | Profitable | Not applicable)

## Market & Competition

5. **What market are we analyzing?** (Geographic scope, industry vertical, customer segment)
6. **Who are the known competitors?** (List them. Include indirect competitors and the status quo.)
7. **What's the hypothesized competitive advantage?** (Why would customers choose this over alternatives?)
8. **What data do we already have?** (Customer interviews, usage data, revenue data, market reports)

## Analysis Goals

9. **What decision does this analysis need to inform?** (Go/no-go? Pricing? Positioning? Fundraising? Hiring? Pivoting?)
10. **Who is the audience for the deliverable?** (Yourself | Co-founders | Investors | Board | Team)
11. **What's the deliverable format?** (Memo | Deck | Spreadsheet model | Decision brief | Full report)
12. **What's the confidence level needed?** (Directional gut-check | Data-informed estimate | Rigorous analysis)

## Financial

13. **Do we need financial modeling?** (Unit economics | Revenue projections | Cash flow | Cap table | None)
14. **What are the key assumptions to test?** (Customer acquisition cost, conversion rate, churn, price point, etc.)
15. **What's the time horizon?** (3 months | 1 year | 3 years | 5 years)

---

## Conditional

### If fundraising
16. **What round?** (Pre-seed | Seed | Series A | Bridge | Other)
17. **How much are you raising?** (Target amount and valuation expectation if any)
18. **What's the pitch narrative?** (Problem → solution → traction → ask? Or another structure?)

### If go-to-market
19. **What channels are being considered?** (Direct sales | Content/inbound | Product-led | Partnerships | Paid acquisition)
20. **What's the initial target segment?** (Who are the first 100 customers specifically?)

### If pivot/turnaround
21. **What triggered the change?** (Metrics decline | Market shift | Customer feedback | Competitive pressure)
22. **What's the constraint on pivoting?** (Existing customers | Technical debt | Runway | Team skills)
