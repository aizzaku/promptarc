# Legal & Compliance Kickoff Checklist

Run after universal checklist Phase 1. Ask 3 questions at a time.

---

## Required

### Work Type & Scope

1. **What type of legal work?**
   - Contract drafting (creating from scratch)
   - Contract review (reviewing an existing document)
   - Policy drafting (internal policy, privacy policy, ToS)
   - Regulatory analysis (understanding what a law/rule requires)
   - Compliance program (building out a compliance function)
   - Legal research (answering a specific legal question)
   - Mixed

2. **What's the jurisdiction?**
   - United States (federal)
   - Specific US state(s) — which?
   - European Union (GDPR-relevant)
   - United Kingdom
   - Canada
   - Multiple jurisdictions — specify
   - Global / jurisdiction-agnostic

3. **What industry or regulatory context?**
   - General commercial / technology
   - Financial services (SEC, FINRA, banking regulators)
   - Healthcare (HIPAA, FDA)
   - Privacy (GDPR, CCPA, CPRA)
   - Employment / HR
   - Real estate
   - Other — specify

---

### Audience & Formality

4. **Who is the audience for this work?**
   - In-house review only (not external-facing)
   - External parties (counterparties, regulators, customers)
   - Board / executives
   - Investors

5. **What's the formality level?**
   - In-house working draft (internal standard)
   - External counsel quality (high precision, no ambiguity)
   - Regulatory submission (formal, citation-heavy)
   - Plain-language summary (for non-lawyers)

6. **Will this be reviewed by licensed counsel before use?**
   - Yes — this is a first draft for attorney review
   - No — this will be used as-is
   - Unknown

---

### Document Context

7. **Does existing material exist to work from?**
   - Yes — provide or describe it
   - No — starting from scratch
   - Yes, a template exists but needs heavy customization

8. **Are there comparable documents or precedents to follow?**
   - Yes — describe them or link
   - No — this is novel territory
   - Standard market practice exists but I don't have a reference

9. **What are the most sensitive points in this work?**
   (e.g., limitation of liability clause, IP ownership, data processing terms, specific regulatory obligations) Free text.

---

## Conditional

### If contract drafting or review

10. **What type of contract?**
    - Master Service Agreement (MSA)
    - NDA / Confidentiality agreement
    - SaaS subscription agreement
    - Employment / contractor agreement
    - Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
    - Vendor / procurement agreement
    - Partnership / JV agreement
    - Other — specify

11. **What's the commercial context?**
    (Deal size, relationship type, negotiating leverage, whether this is standard or negotiated)

12. **Which party's interests does this protect?** (Your position in the deal)

---

### If policy drafting

13. **What's triggering this policy?** (Regulatory requirement, incident response, investor due diligence, proactive hygiene)

14. **Who needs to comply with this policy?** (Employees, vendors, customers, all of the above)

15. **Does it need to be enforceable?** (Some policies are guidance; others need to hold up in court or a regulatory audit)

---

### If regulatory analysis

16. **What specific regulation, statute, or rule is in scope?**

17. **What's the practical question this analysis needs to answer?** (Not "what does GDPR say" but "does our data processing agreement need to include X clause given Y fact pattern")

18. **What's the decision this analysis will inform?**
