# Sales / GTM Kickoff Checklist

Run after universal checklist Phase 1. Ask 3 questions at a time.

---

## Required

### Motion & Segment

1. **What's the sales motion?**
   - Inbound (leads come to you — content, SEO, product-led)
   - Outbound (you go to leads — cold email, LinkedIn, calls)
   - Product-led growth (free tier, trial, self-serve upgrade)
   - Channel / partnership (resellers, integrations, co-sell)
   - Mixed — specify which motions and for which segments

2. **What deal type / segment?**
   - SMB / self-serve (< $5K ACV, low touch)
   - Mid-market ($5K–$100K ACV, some sales involvement)
   - Enterprise ($100K+ ACV, full sales cycle)
   - Transactional (volume, no defined ACV)

3. **What's the ICP?** (Ideal Customer Profile — be specific)
   Not "B2B tech companies." Closer to: "Series B–D SaaS companies with 50–200 engineers who are scaling their infra team and have a dedicated DevOps function but no platform team yet."

---

### Pipeline Stage & Goal

4. **Where in the funnel does this work live?**
   - Top of funnel (awareness, lead generation, outbound prospecting)
   - Mid-funnel (qualification, discovery, demos, proposals)
   - Closing (negotiation, objection handling, procurement)
   - Expansion / renewal (upsell, cross-sell, retention)
   - Full funnel

5. **What's the immediate goal of this work?**
   - Book meetings (from cold)
   - Move deals forward (stuck in pipeline)
   - Close a specific deal
   - Build a repeatable sequence / playbook
   - Define ICP and positioning
   - Other — specify

6. **What does success look like in 30 days?** (Concrete: X meetings booked, Y demo-to-proposal conversion, Z deals closed)

---

### Messaging & Competitive Context

7. **What does your product actually do?** (In plain English — not the marketing version)

8. **Who are the main competitors, and how do you differentiate?** (Include "doing nothing / status quo" as a competitor)

9. **What's the primary pain you solve?** (One specific pain, not a category. "Sales teams waste 4 hours/week manually updating CRM records" beats "improve sales efficiency.")

---

## Conditional

### If outbound sequences

10. **What channels?** (Cold email / LinkedIn / phone / all three)

11. **Does an existing sequence exist?** (If yes: what's the open rate, reply rate, meeting rate? What's working and what isn't?)

12. **How personalized is this?** (Hyper-personalized 1:1 / persona-personalized / templatized at scale)

---

### If mid-to-late stage (demos, proposals, closing)

13. **What are the top 3 objections you hear most?** (Not hypothetical — actual objections from real calls)

14. **Who are the buyer personas in a typical deal?** (Economic buyer, technical evaluator, champion, blocker — and who holds veto power)

15. **What does the approval/procurement process look like on the buyer side?** (Legal, InfoSec, finance, executive sign-off — and typical timelines for each)

---

### If building a GTM strategy / playbook

16. **What's the current state?** (No playbook / informal playbook / formal but not followed / playbook exists but needs updating)

17. **What's the sales team size and structure?** (SDRs, AEs, SEs, CSMs — and who owns what stage)

18. **What data exists?** (CRM data, win/loss analysis, customer interviews, cohort performance by segment)
